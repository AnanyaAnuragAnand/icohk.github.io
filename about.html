The International Centre of Humanism, Kanpur (ICOHK) is registered under the Societies Registration Act, 1860. It has been actively involved in providing healthcare services, particularly in rural and underserved communities.

ICOHK aligns with the principles of the WHO Alma Ata Declaration (1978), emphasizing primary healthcare and the goal of “Health for All” through community participation.
